 SEO Considerations from Your Current Sitemap:

    Homepage as Primary Calculator: Your homepage (https://prostatevolumecalc.com/) being the Prostate Volume Calculator itself is a specific choice. This can work, especially if direct traffic to the domain expects this calculator. However, for broader SEO and user experience, a dedicated homepage that acts as a portal is often more effective. I will structure with your current approach but also offer a suggestion if you want to evolve.
    URL Redundancy/Alternatives: You have several URLs that might point to similar content or are variations (e.g., /calculator, /prostate-volume-calculator vs. /; /tools vs. /advanced-tools; /psa-density vs. /psa-density-calculator). This can cause duplicate content issues and dilute SEO. You should choose ONE canonical URL for each piece of content and 301 redirect the alternatives to it.

    <mobile:mobile/> Tag: This tag is deprecated by Google. A responsive design is the standard and preferred approach.
